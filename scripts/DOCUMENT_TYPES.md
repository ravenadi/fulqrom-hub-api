# Generated Document Types Reference

This document describes all the document types generated by the `generateDummyData.js` script.

## Document Categories Overview

The script generates **5 categories** of documents across different entity types:

### 1. Building Compliance Documents
**Quantity:** 3 per building

#### Fire Safety Certificate
- **Category:** Compliance
- **Type:** Certificate
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site, Building
- **Special fields:**
  - Regulatory Framework: "Building Code of Australia"
  - Compliance Status: "Compliant"
  - Issue Date: 60 days ago
  - Expiry Date: 305 days from now (approx. 1 year from issue)
  - Review Date: 275 days from now
- **Tags:** compliance, fire-safety, building, certificate
- **Purpose:** Annual fire safety compliance certificate as per Australian Standards

#### Electrical Safety Certificate
- **Category:** Compliance
- **Type:** Certificate
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site, Building
- **Special fields:**
  - Regulatory Framework: "AS/NZS 3000:2018"
  - Compliance Status: "Compliant"
  - Issue Date: 90 days ago
  - Expiry Date: 275 days from now
- **Tags:** compliance, electrical, safety, certificate
- **Purpose:** Electrical installation and testing certificate

#### As-Built Plans
- **Category:** Drawing Register
- **Type:** Plan
- **Status:** Approved
- **Version:** 2.1
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site, Building
- **Drawing Info:**
  - Date Issued: 180 days ago
  - Drawing Status: "approved"
  - Prepared By: "ABC Architecture Pty Ltd"
  - Drawing Scale: "1:100"
  - Approved By: "Senior Architect"
- **Tags:** drawing, as-built, architectural, engineering
- **Purpose:** Complete as-built architectural and engineering drawings

---

### 2. Asset Documentation
**Quantity:** Variable (sample of up to 20 assets)

#### Asset Manuals
- **Category:** Technical
- **Type:** Manual
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site, Building, Floor, Asset (full hierarchy)
- **Tags:** manual, asset, [category-specific]
- **Purpose:** Operation and maintenance manual for specific equipment
- **Example:** "Chiller System Manual - A-12345"

#### Warranty Certificates
- **Category:** Legal
- **Type:** Certificate
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site, Building, Asset
- **Condition:** Only generated for assets installed within last 5 years
- **Special fields:**
  - Issue Date: Asset installation date
  - Expiry Date: 5 years from installation
- **Tags:** warranty, certificate, asset
- **Purpose:** Manufacturer warranty certificate

---

### 3. Vendor Contracts
**Quantity:** 1 per vendor (5 total)

#### Service Agreements
- **Category:** Legal
- **Type:** Contract
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Vendor
- **Special fields:**
  - Issue Date: 180 days ago
  - Expiry Date: 545 days from now (approx. 2 years from issue)
  - Review Date: 455 days from now
- **Tags:** contract, vendor, legal, service-agreement
- **Purpose:** Master service agreement for contractor services
- **Example:** "Service Agreement - Summit Properties Services"

---

### 4. Site-level Documents
**Quantity:** 2 per site

#### Site Safety Management Plan
- **Category:** Operations
- **Type:** Plan
- **Status:** Approved
- **Version:** 2.0
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site
- **Special fields:**
  - Issue Date: 365 days ago (1 year)
  - Review Date: 365 days from now (annual review)
  - Frequency: "annual"
- **Tags:** safety, operations, site, emergency
- **Purpose:** Comprehensive safety management plan and emergency procedures

#### Environmental Management Plan
- **Category:** Compliance
- **Type:** Plan
- **Status:** Approved
- **Linked to:** Customer, Site
- **Special fields:**
  - Regulatory Framework: "EPA Guidelines"
  - Compliance Status: "Compliant"
- **Tags:** environment, compliance, sustainability, site
- **Purpose:** Environmental compliance and sustainability management plan

---

### 5. Technical Reports
**Quantity:** Variable (sample of up to 10 assets)

#### Maintenance Reports
- **Category:** Technical
- **Type:** Report
- **Status:** Approved

- **Special fields:**

## Document Associations

### Location Hierarchy
Documents are linked to different levels:

```
Customer (All documents)
└── Site
    ├── Site Safety Plans
    ├── Environmental Plans
    └── Building
        ├── Fire Safety Certificates
        ├── Electrical Certificates
        ├── As-Built Plans
        └── Floor
            └── Assets
                ├── Manuals
                ├── Warranties
                └── Maintenance Reports
```

### Vendor Association
```
Vendor
└── Service Agreements (linked to customer)
```

## Compliance Features

### Certificates with Expiry Tracking
These documents include expiry dates for compliance tracking:
- Fire Safety Certificates (annual)
- Electrical Safety Certificates (annual)
- Vendor Service Agreements (2 years)
- Asset Warranties (5 years from installation)

### Regulatory Frameworks
- **Building Code of Australia** - Fire Safety
- **AS/NZS 3000:2018** - Electrical Safety
- **EPA Guidelines** - Environmental Management

### Review Frequencies
- **Annual:** Site Safety Plans, Fire Safety Certificates
- **As-needed:** Technical Reports, Maintenance Reports
- **Contract-based:** Vendor Agreements

- Under Review

- Archived

## Notes

- All documents are **metadata only** - no actual files are created
- File attachment fields (file_url, file_path, file_key) are empty
- Dates are calculated relative to current date for realistic scenarios
- All data follows Australian standards and naming conventions
- Document names include entity identifiers for easy recognition
